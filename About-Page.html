ABOUT SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL DEPARTMENT

Our Lady of Lourdes College Foundation Senior High School Department is a Catholic educational institution that offers senior high school programs to students. It is located in Daet, Camarines Norte, Philippines.

 

For more information about Our Lady of Lourdes College Foundation Senior High School Department, you can visit their official website: https://ollcf.edu.ph/
